Our Story

In the heart of the city, where the scent of charred oak meets the crisp evening air, Slice of Heaven was born from a simple obsession: the perfect crust. Our journey began with a single hand-built brick oven and a commitment to the ancient Neapolitan art of pizza making.

Every dough is fermented for 48 hours, every tomato is hand-crushed, and every flame is carefully tended. We don't just cook; we honor the tradition of the hearth.
